# Advisor Review Guide

Advisors review EBF scorecards for rubric fitness, evidence quality, calibration consistency, and claim safety.

## Review Scope

- Confirm the seven EBF pillars are scored with the active rubric version.
- Confirm farm-specific metric profiles are justified when defaults do not fit local context.
- Confirm score evidence links are sufficient for the intended use.
- Confirm public scorecards meet Level 4 evidence maturity or higher.
- Confirm public carbon pillar scores meet Level 6 evidence maturity.
- Confirm stakeholder feedback is aggregated or public-consented before use in public equity narratives.

## Calibration

Third-party calibration is preferred. If calibration is performed by the Kokonut team, require a calibration report URL or report hash before verification or publication.

Recommended cadence:

- Semi-annual for pilot farms.
- Annual for network farms.
- Additional review after major methodology, land-use, or evidence-source changes.

## Output

Advisors may provide:

- calibration notes
- suggested score adjustments
- evidence gap recommendations
- rubric change recommendations
- publication caveats

Advisor recommendations are not publication authority. Final verification and publication remain governed human workflow actions.
